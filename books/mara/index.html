Mara

translesbianscience fiction

​Mara is available to purchase on itch.io or other storefronts.

“Cinematic. Wild ride. Left me ravenous for more.” – Tris Husband

A trio of political factions stretches across the universe, claiming to keep all in their grasp safe. To cement their role as protector and spiritual guide, they have at last created their crowning achievement, a breakthrough of faith and technology: the android, Mara.

What no one expected was Mara's escape to carve her own path. Now, the most powerful forces in the Universe are bearing down on her. They will do everything to stop the android they created from upsetting the tenuous peace of the civilised universe.

Pursued by both captors and followers alike, Mara unwaveringly chases her own purpose. A mission as mysterious as it is potent.

Publishing in August 2026 by Prismata Press – Tāmaki Makaurau, Aotearoa.
