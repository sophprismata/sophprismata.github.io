Prismata Press

Nestled among the volcanoes of New Zealand, Prismata Press is a fiery trans-owned and operated micro-press with a passion for sharing trans voices. We publish fiction and poetry in e-book, print, and audio formats. With a strong background in the trans indie fiction scene, we are always ready to lift up queer voices.
